**Mission:** Kalimu exists to make learning personal, accessible, and empowering for every student. We believe education should adapt to the learner—not the other way around.

## Project Overview

Kalimu is an EdTech platform designed to replace one-size-fits-all education with personalized, skill-based learning roadmaps. It helps learners identify where they are, where they want to be, and the most effective path to get there. Whether it’s mastering a new subject or preparing for a career, Kalimu aligns resources, goals, and progress tracking into a single, student-centered experience.

## Why It Matters

In many schools and training programs, students are forced to move at the same pace, with the same materials, regardless of their starting point. This creates gaps for some and boredom for others. Kalimu flips that model by giving each learner a roadmap built for their unique skills, goals, and preferred learning style. The result is a more engaging, motivating, and effective learning journey.

## Our Approach

### Personalized Learning at Scale

Kalimu generates customized learning paths that adapt in real-time based on a student’s progress, strengths, and needs.

### Skill Mapping & Goal Setting

We help learners break big ambitions into achievable steps, while pinpointing exactly which skills they need to work on next.

### Accessible & Inclusive Design

The platform is built to work for anyone, anywhere—making quality learning guidance available beyond traditional classrooms.

## Early Impact

* Helped pilot group students improve skill mastery speed by an average of **30%** through tailored resource recommendations
* Increased student engagement through visual, milestone-based progress tracking
* Reduced dropout in test group by providing dynamic, flexible roadmaps that adjust when learners fall behind
