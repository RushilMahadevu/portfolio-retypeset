# Strides Over Stigma: Building a Platform for Mental Health Action

Mental health stigma thrives in silence. I wanted to change that, so I founded **Strides Over Stigma**—a wellness initiative and full-stack web platform designed to make mental health support accessible and community-driven.

## Why Start Strides Over Stigma?

Too many people struggle alone, afraid to speak up. I saw the need for a space where honest conversation and real support could happen, both online and in person. The platform I built lets users register for events, share stories, and connect with resources—all with privacy and empathy at the core.

## Building the Platform

I used TypeScript and React to create a seamless experience for event registration and community outreach. The biggest challenge was designing features that encourage participation without sacrificing user safety or comfort.

## Community Impact

Since launch, Strides Over Stigma has hosted multiple events and helped dozens of people find support. The feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, with users highlighting the sense of belonging and the practical tools for wellness.

## What Comes Next

I'm working on expanding the platform's reach and partnering with local organizations to bring more resources to the community. The mission is simple: make it easier for people to get help and for stigma to lose its power.
